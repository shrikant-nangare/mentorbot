## Regional rehearsal + Zoom checklist (middle school division)

Supervising adult:
- Present and visible the entire time
- Silent during the interview (middle school rule)

---

## A) 5 timed rehearsals (required)
Goal: a consistent **5:00 ± 0:10** pitch with no rushed demo.

Rehearsal rules:
- Screen-share exactly like the real interview.
- Use the exact script in `REGIONAL_DEMO_SCRIPT.md`.
- If anything breaks, practice the fallback line and continue.

Common “time leaks” to cut:
- Typing questions live → copy/paste the demo prompt.
- Over-explaining architecture → keep it for Q&A/backup slides.
- Clicking too much in Notes → show scroll + delete once, then close.

---

## B) Record a 30–45s backup demo clip (highly recommended)
Purpose: if network/LLM fails during the interview, you can play the clip while narrating.

Clip content (30–45s):
- Student selects profile → asks concept question → quiz appears → show “Submit”.
- Open Notes → show “Recent notes” scroll + delete.

How to record:
- Use macOS screen record (QuickTime) or Zoom “record locally”.
- Keep the cursor movements slow and intentional.
- Do NOT show any keys/passwords.

Where to embed:
- Add it as a hidden/backup slide after the live demo slide.

---

## C) Zoom day-of checklist (10–15 minutes early)
Join waiting room 10–15 minutes early and run:
- **Audio**: correct microphone, input level, no echo.
- **Video**: camera framing, lighting, background.
- **Screen share**: confirm you can share the correct window/tab.
- **Browser**: close extra tabs; disable notifications.
- **Credentials**: parent PIN and student PINs known (don’t say them out loud).

Tab setup:
- Tab 1: Student UI at profile picker
- Tab 2: Parent view ready
- Tab 3: Backup slides (`REGIONAL_BACKUP_SLIDES.md`)
- Optional: `/docs` tab for technical proof

---

## D) Q&A drill (10 minutes)
Pick 10 random questions from `REGIONAL_QA_BANK.md`:
- Answer in 20–30 seconds.
- Then offer the “technical follow-up” only if asked.

---

## E) “If it breaks” responses (memorize)
- **LLM error**: “If the model is unavailable, the app still keeps student accounts, quizzes, and notes. The system is provider-agnostic, so we can swap providers. We’ll continue with the product flow and reports.”
- **Slow demo**: “To stay on time, we’ll show the key loop and then take questions.”
